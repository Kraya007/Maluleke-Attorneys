About Us

At J.G. Attorneys, we are more than just a law firm—we are your trusted legal advisors, committed to protecting your rights and guiding you through complex legal processes. Based in Protea North, South Africa, we have built a strong reputation for delivering professional, reliable, and results-driven legal services to individuals, families, and businesses in our community.

Our team specializes in a wide range of legal matters, including estate administration, claims against the state, civil and criminal trials, contract drafting, divorces, maintenance disputes, Road Accident Fund (RAF) claims, pension claims, interdicts, evictions, and more.

At J.G. Attorneys, we understand that each client’s situation is unique. That’s why we take the time to listen, assess your needs, and develop tailored legal strategies to achieve the best possible outcomes.
